People have a misconception that all the characters are completely unique.

Really, there are only around 214 (inb4 "only") basic shapes, called radicals, and the rest of the characters are all combinations.

Characters will often have a radical relating to their meaning on the left side or the top. For example, the characters for "think," "fear," and "sadness" would all have the "heart" radical in them.

The second part of the character will then have a component chosen for its pronunciation.

In other words, you can often get clues to meaning and pronunciation once you know how to look, and these patterns also help with remembering characters.
